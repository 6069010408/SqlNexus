Exporting Reports

 

To export a report, simply click the Export button on the Report toolbar.  You can select from several formats:  Excel, Adobe Acrobat (PDF), JPEG, GIF, Bitmap, TIFF, PNG, and Windows EMF. 

 

Note that child reports are not automatically exported.  To export a child report, drill into it from a top-level report, then click the Export button while viewing it.  Subreports will automatically export with their host top-level report.  Child reports must be exported separately.
